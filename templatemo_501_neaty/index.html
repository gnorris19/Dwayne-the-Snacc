About Mr. Johnson

Dwayne was born on May 2nd, 1972 in Hayward, California. He grew up watching his father wrestle professionally, but didn't take up the sport himself until 1996, focusing instead on football.

He was incredibly successful as a wrestler. He won 16 WWE matches and over 250 WWF matches. Before The Rock became the name that stuck, he was known as Flex Kavanah, Rocky Maivia, and The Peoples' Champion.

Dwayne's acting career began in 2001 when he took time off from wrestling to film his first movie, The Mummy Returns. Since then, he has appeared in many more films and gradually became recognized more as an actor than a wrestler, though he is still active in his sport. Some of his most iconic movies that we remember today are The Game Plan, The Race to Witch Mountain, and The Tooth Fairy.
